Shivani's Rice and Yogurt (Curd Rice)

Credit: Indian Veggie Delight and Bhavana Patil

Description

This South Indian staple provides warmth and comfort with common household ingredients, and comes together in just a few minutes.

Ingredients list

  • 1.5 cups freshly cooked rice
  • 2-3 tablespoons full-fat yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a small bowl and serve immediately.
